Booking a bus ticket online in the Philippines is easy with GRAN Turismo. Simply enter the Leaving from -- Going to destination details along with the date you wish to travel in the bus search option on the site. Within seconds you will be given a list of buses availability for your route. Select your bus that suits you best for you. Then just follow the simple steps to the ticket booking payment process and your seat will be reserved for your bus journey.

Most of the online bus bookings are more than 50% unplanned, with 32% of the people reserving tickets on the day of travel and 24% one day before the journey. Keeping this in mind, GRAN Turismo has ensured buses are available round the clock 24/7.
