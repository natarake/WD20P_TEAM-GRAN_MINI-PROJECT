Our Story

GRAN Turismo, over the years, has strived to deliver easy booking solutions to its customers. Our continued efforts have resulted into GRAN Turismo becoming one of the leading ticketing platforms in the Philippines for various Bus services. We have a strong presence with a ticket inventory from over 2500 bus partners and 100000 route options our app and website. Our

  • 2008 - GRAN Turismo launches with a new consumer website and UI. With inventory of over 300,000 tickets to offer per day, GRAN Turismo became the Philippines' biggest ticketing project.
  • 2016 - GRAN Turismo VIP is launched, a members exclusive service. Rentals service is launched for systematic online renting of buses.
  • 2022 - GRANpay, GRAN Turismo's own wallet, launched. First bus ticketing company to provide wallet to its customers

Today, GRAN Turismo is also the preferred Information Technology Partner for Land Transportation and Regularatory Board and Philippine Transport Cooperative. GRAN Turismo is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company.
